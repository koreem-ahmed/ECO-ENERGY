Our Projects

Our proven track record showcases successful renewable energy installations across diverse environments, demonstrating technical excellence and environmental impact through innovative wind, solar, and hybrid energy solutions.

Wind Energy Project

Advanced wind turbine installation featuring 3.5MW capacity with aerodynamic blade design. This project generates clean electricity for 2,800 homes while reducing CO2 emissions by 8,500 tons annually.

Traditional Wind Mill Integration

Historic windmill restoration combining traditional architecture with modern renewable energy technology. This heritage project demonstrates sustainable development while preserving cultural landmarks and generating renewable electricity for local communities.

Urban Solar Installation

Rooftop solar array system installed on commercial buildings in urban environments. This project utilizes advanced photovoltaic technology to provide clean energy while reducing building operational costs and carbon footprint.

Ground-Mounted Solar Farm

Large-scale solar installation featuring ground-mounted panels with optimal tilt angles for maximum energy capture. This project generates renewable electricity for 1,500 homes while preventing 3,200 tons of annual CO2 emissions.

Fixed-Tilt Solar Array

Utility-scale solar installation with fixed-tilt mounting systems designed for optimal year-round performance. This project utilizes high-efficiency monocrystalline panels to deliver consistent clean energy output with minimal maintenance requirements.

Artistic Solar Installation

Innovative solar panel design integrating renewable energy technology with architectural aesthetics. This project demonstrates creative approaches to sustainable energy while maintaining visual appeal and generating clean electricity for community facilities.
